## FINALIZATION
**PROTOCOL: Update registry and commit completion.**

1.  **Update Status:** In the **Tracks Registry**, change the status of the track to `[x]`.
2.  **Reorganize:** Move the entire track section from its current location to the bottom of the `## Closed` section.
3.  **Commit:** Stage the **Tracks Registry** and commit with `chore(_spec): Mark track '<track_description>' as complete`.
