## SPECIFICATION GENERATION
**PROTOCOL: Build spec.md interactively.**

1.  **State Goal:** "I'll now guide you through a series of questions to build a comprehensive specification (`spec.md`) for this track."
2.  **Questioning Phase:**
    - Ask questions sequentially (one by one).
    - Classify as **Additive** (Select all that apply) or **Exclusive Choice**.
    - Provide 2-3 options plus "Type your own answer".
    - FEATURE: 3-5 questions.
    - BUG/CHORE: 2-3 questions.
3.  **Draft:** Once gathered, draft `spec.md` (Overview, Requirements, Acceptance Criteria, Out of Scope).
4.  **Confirm:** Present to user for approval/revision.
